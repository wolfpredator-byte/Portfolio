THE JOURNEY

With over 3 years of experience in the digital arts, my journey began in the world of graphic design before transitioning into the limitless potential of 3D space. I believe that every pixel should tell a story, and every shadow should hide a secret.

My philosophy is rooted in technical precision. I don't just "render" scenes; I simulate physics, optics, and material science to ensure that every refraction and subsurface scatter feels grounded in the laws of nature.
